Welcome to **Recoupable Research**, where we dive deep into the intersection of music, technology, and data.

## What We'll Cover

At Recoupable, we're building infrastructure for the next generation of independent artists. Through this blog, we'll share:

- **Industry Analysis**: Data-driven insights into music streaming, fan engagement, and revenue trends
- **Product Deep Dives**: How we're building tools to help artists understand and grow their audiences
- **Technology Explorations**: Emerging tech in music (AI, blockchain, social platforms)
- **Case Studies**: Real stories from artists using data to make better decisions

## Why Research Matters

The music industry is undergoing massive transformation. Artists now have more tools than ever, but also more noise to cut through. Our mission is to bring clarity through:

1. **Data Analysis** - Turning streaming numbers and social metrics into actionable insights
2. **Tool Building** - Creating products that actually help artists make money
3. **Knowledge Sharing** - Publishing what we learn along the way

## Code Examples

As a technical team, we'll often share code snippets:

```typescript
// Example: Calculating artist growth rate
function calculateGrowthRate(current: number, previous: number): number {
  return ((current - previous) / previous) * 100;
}

const monthlyGrowth = calculateGrowthRate(10000, 8500);
console.log(`Monthly growth: ${monthlyGrowth.toFixed(2)}%`); // 17.65%
```
